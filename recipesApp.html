Cook Like a Chef

Chef's Recipe Collection and Cooking App

Overview

In recent years, the culinary world has seen a remarkable surge in creativity and innovation. With an abundance of diverse recipes and cooking techniques originating from various regions, there is a compelling need for centralization.

This project aims to address this need by developing a user-friendly and accessible culinary application concept, catering to individuals of all skill levels. This concept offers a curated collection of chef-approved recipes, spanning both sophisticated and straightforward cooking methods. Each recipe provides comprehensive details, including ingredient lists and preparation instructions, with a strong emphasis on the visual presentation of the dishes, recognizing the crucial role it plays in inspiring and guiding aspiring chefs. It's important to note that this concept has not been fully developed.

Implementation methods

Choice of Technology
This application is developed using the Flutter framework and the Dart programming language. The choice of these technologies is driven by their advantages in enabling versatile platforms and fast development for a robust culinary application.

API-Driven Data Retrieval and Data Manipulation
The approach is based on data retrieval through APIs, enabling us to fetch data from custom-built APIs. These data are then harnessed using various filtering methods and employed in diverse ways within the application.

Key Features

Renowned chefs are categorized into "chategory bundles," and through the fetching of recipes from the API, these recipes are sorted into their respective categories. This allows users to easily find recipes from their desired chef. As a result, a list displaying the main details of each dish is presented, including a visual representation. By clicking on these visuals, users are transported to a detailed preparation screen, providing comprehensive cooking instructions and additional information.

Additionally, users have access to search and filtering functionalities, allowing them to efficiently explore recipes based on ingredients and titles, facilitating the discovery of suitable dishes.

Future Development

This project was initiated in the late summer of 2023, driven by a passion for culinary arts and the need to consolidate beloved recipes in one place. Additionally, it served as an opportunity to acquire proficiency in a versatile programming language. With these considerations in mind, there are several plans for the continued development of the application, including:

  • Establishing Server Connectivity
  • Favorites and Completed Recipes
  • Allergen Filtering System
  • Meal Planning and Shopping Lists
  • Multi-Language Support
