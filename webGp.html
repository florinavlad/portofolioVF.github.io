Greenpanion

Web application for facilitating the recycling process

Overview

By 2020, Romania was supposed to reach a recycling rate of 50% to avoid penalties from the European Union. The next threshold of 55% must be achieved by 2025. The recycling rate places the country at the bottom of the European Union, which is a major reason to contribute towards change.

The primary objective is to support current and future initiatives for promoting and enhancing waste recycling with the intention of increasing community involvement in this ecological endeavor. On the web page, users can view the locations of recycling centers, information about rewards, the points needed for winning, and their own statistics if they are logged in. Users can only accumulate points by scanning or manually entering unique codes provided by automated recycling machines located at various centers, following the recycling of materials within the categories of plastic, metal, and paper.

Implementation methods

In order to reach as many internet users as possible, a complementary web application has been chosen to provide information and guide users towards downloading the mobile app for additional functionalities.
Android Studio, coupled with the Kotlin programming language, was used to meet the requirements of the mobile application, while ReactJS was used for the web page. Data manipulation and user interface communication were achieved using Spring Boot, and PostgreSQL was employed for database management.

Registration and Authentication

The registration and login forms are created using the Formik library, simplifying form state management and the handling of external actions. To enforce desired constraints, the YUP library is included to address cases where fields are left incomplete or unselected before submission. Additionally, for email and password validation, regex constraints for predefined rules are introduced. Once authenticated, users are allowed to navigate the leaderboard by viewing statistics of active accounts.

Map and Prizes

In order to implement the display of recycling centers in a user-friendly manner, an interactive map is developed by integrating the Google Maps API and creating a corresponding marker for each recycling center based on its latitude and longitude.
Awards are presented in the form of a suggestive photo carousel for each item, along with the points required for winning through material recycling.

Usability and Responsiveness

Users have the ability to navigate the application easily and access available functionalities in a coherent and trouble-free manner. Design elements are responsive to provide a pleasant experience regardless of the device categories used. To maintain a cohesive user experience, design elements, such as color schemes, typography, and layout, are consistent throughout the application. This consistency not only enhances usability but also reinforces brand identity.

Future Development

The website has the potential to become much more interactive and useful, in addition to its promotional benefits, although the main action is represented by the Android application. This heartfelt project has the following future development objectives: Inclusion of a suggestive demo of the Android mobile application's usage, representation of the locations associated with existing accounts by adding markers on the map, and inclusion of authentic articles regarding the recycling situation in Romania to enhance motivation.
